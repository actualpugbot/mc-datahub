## Design Context

### Users
Internal developers and data maintainers use `mc-datahub` to inspect extracted Minecraft datasets, compare versions, and verify local outputs against reference sources such as minecraft.wiki.

### Brand Personality
Utilitarian, dependable, grounded. Interfaces should feel calm and clear, with the emphasis on confidence, scanability, and quick verification rather than flourish.

### Aesthetic Direction
Simple light-theme tooling with restrained earthy Minecraft-adjacent tones, obvious controls, and dense but readable information. Avoid playful marketing polish, heavy motion, and decorative UI patterns that get in the way of comparison work.

### Design Principles
- Make version comparison obvious before making it beautiful.
- Keep extracted data and reference data visibly separated.
- Prefer searchable, scannable lists over hidden interactions.
- Use detail-on-demand for long sound lists.
- Treat missing coverage and mismatches as first-class signals.
